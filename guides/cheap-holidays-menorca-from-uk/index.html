Cheap Holidays To Menorca From The UK

1 min read

Menorca is the quieter, more relaxed sister of Mallorca and Ibiza, making it perfect for families and couples seeking a peaceful budget holiday. Known for its turquoise bays (calas) and gentle pace of life, it offers a high quality of life at a reasonable price.

Accommodation in resorts like Cala en Porter and Son Bou offers excellent value, while the two main cities, Mahon and Ciutadella, are perfect for exploring on foot.

Cheapest Time To Visit Menorca

The cheapest months to visit Menorca from the UK are:

  • May and June
  • September and October

The island is beautiful in late spring when the wildflowers are in bloom and the beaches are quiet.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £45–£130 return
  • Hotels: £50–£100 per night
  • Local gin & lemonade (Pomada): £5

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Rent a car for a few days to reach the remote, free beaches (Calas)
  • Shop at local supermarkets for lunch and beach picnics
  • Visit the historic sites of Mahon and Ciutadella for free or low costs
  • Look for 'hostals'—these are often high-quality, family-run guesthouses
