Cheap Holidays To Kos From The UK

1 min read

Kos is a well-rounded Greek island that offers great value for budget travellers. With long sandy beaches, interesting historic sites, and a wide choice of package holidays, it's an easy and affordable getaway from the UK.

Kardamena is known for its affordable nightlife and budget hotels, while Kos Town offers a mix of history and modern amenities at reasonable prices.

Cheapest Time To Visit Kos

The cheapest months to visit Kos from the UK are:

  • May
  • October

These months offer the best balance of warm weather and low costs, especially for last-minute bookings.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Leeds Bradford

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £55–£150 return
  • Hotels: £30–£60 per night
  • Bicycle rental (day): £5

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Kos is very flat—rent a bicycle as a cheap and healthy way to get around
  • Visit the free thermal springs at Embros Therme
  • Eat at local tavernas away from the main harbour in Kos Town
  • Look for 'all-in' deals in Kardamena for great package value
