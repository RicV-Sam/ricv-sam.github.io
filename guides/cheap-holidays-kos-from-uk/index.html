Cheap Holidays To Kos From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Kos is a well-rounded Greek island that offers great value for budget travellers. With long sandy beaches, interesting historic sites, and a wide choice of package holidays, it's an easy and affordable getaway from the UK.

Kardamena is known for its affordable nightlife and budget hotels, while Kos Town offers a mix of history and modern amenities at reasonable prices.

Cheapest Time To Visit Kos

The cheapest months to visit Kos from the UK are:

  • May
  • October

These months offer the best balance of warm weather and low costs, especially for last-minute bookings.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Leeds Bradford

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £55–£150 return
  • Hotels: £30–£60 per night
  • Bicycle rental (day): £5

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Kos is very flat—rent a bicycle as a cheap and healthy way to get around
  • Visit the free thermal springs at Embros Therme
  • Eat at local tavernas away from the main harbour in Kos Town
  • Look for 'all-in' deals in Kardamena for great package value

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Kos from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Kos is typically during May and October, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Kos cost from the UK?

Flights: £55–£150 return. Hotels: £30–£60 per night. Bicycle rental (day): £5

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Kos?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Kos?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Kos holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Kos due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
