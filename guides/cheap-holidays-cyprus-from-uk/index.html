Cheap Holidays To Cyprus From The UK

1 min read

Cyprus is one of the most popular budget holiday destinations for UK travellers, offering reliable weather, competitive package deals, and excellent value accommodation. Popular resorts such as Paphos, Larnaca, Ayia Napa, and Limassol regularly offer affordable package holidays from major UK airports.

With its combination of stunning Mediterranean coastline and a wealth of history, Cyprus provides a high-quality holiday experience without the premium price tag. Package prices typically range between £400–£600, making it an accessible choice for families and couples alike.

Cheapest Time To Visit Cyprus

The cheapest months to visit Cyprus from the UK are:

  • April–June
  • September–November

These months offer lower hotel prices and cheaper flights compared to peak summer.

Cheapest UK Airports

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £70–£200 return
  • Hotels: £40–£90 per night
  • Package holidays: £300–£600

Budget Tips

  • Book 6–10 weeks early
  • Avoid peak August
  • Use comparison sites
  • Travel midweek
