Cheap Holidays To Cyprus From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Cyprus is one of the most popular budget holiday destinations for UK travellers, offering reliable weather, competitive package deals, and excellent value accommodation. Popular resorts such as Paphos, Larnaca, Ayia Napa, and Limassol regularly offer affordable package holidays from major UK airports.

With its combination of stunning Mediterranean coastline and a wealth of history, Cyprus provides a high-quality holiday experience without the premium price tag. Package prices typically range between £400–£600, making it an accessible choice for families and couples alike.

Cheapest Time To Visit Cyprus

The cheapest months to visit Cyprus from the UK are:

  • April–June
  • September–November

These months offer lower hotel prices and cheaper flights compared to peak summer.

Cheapest UK Airports

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £70–£200 return
  • Hotels: £40–£90 per night
  • Package holidays: £300–£600

Budget Tips

  • Book 6–10 weeks early
  • Avoid peak August
  • Use comparison sites
  • Travel midweek

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Cyprus from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Cyprus is typically during April–June and September–November, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Cyprus cost from the UK?

Flights: £70–£200 return. Hotels: £40–£90 per night. Package holidays: £300–£600

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Cyprus?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Cyprus?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Cyprus holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Cyprus due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
