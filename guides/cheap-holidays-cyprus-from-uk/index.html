Cheap Holidays To Cyprus From The UK

3 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

For many UK holidaymakers, Cyprus represents the perfect blend of exotic allure and wallet-friendly practicality. If you enjoy Mediterranean islands, you might also consider budget travel in Mallorca. From the moment you arrive to see the legendary Aphrodite's Rock or wander around the historic Tombs of the Kings, you'll realize why this has become such a popular choice for those looking to maximize their travel budget. With frequent flights and diverse package options departing from regional UK airports, the journey to Cyprus is both convenient and surprisingly economical.

What truly sets Cyprus apart is the wealth of low-cost activities and dining options available. You can spend your days visiting the vibrant Nicosia Old City or simply soaking in the local atmosphere, knowing that your money goes much further here than in typical European capitals. If you enjoy Mediterranean island life, you might also like budget breaks in Rhodes. The cheapest months to visit Cyprus from the UK are:

  • April–June

Best Areas to Stay in Cyprus for Budget Travellers

When it comes to finding affordable lodging in Cyprus, location is everything. Savvy travellers often bypass the expensive city centres in favour of districts like the tourist hub of Paphos, where you can find much better value for your money. These neighbourhoods usually boast excellent transport links, making it a breeze to commute to the main sights while enjoying a quieter, more local environment at night.

Areas such as the lively Ayia Napa and the coastal Limassol are particularly well-suited for those watching their pennies. Here, the concentration of budget-friendly hostels and simple apartments is higher, and the local dining scene reflects the more modest prices of the residents. Staying in these parts of Cyprus allows you to stretch your budget further, giving you more freedom to spend on experiences and attractions rather than just a place to sleep.

These months offer lower hotel prices and cheaper flights compared to peak summer.

Typical Daily Travel Costs in Cyprus

While your total spend in Cyprus will vary based on your travel style, most budget-conscious visitors find that £50 and £80 a day is a realistic target. This budget allows for a clean and safe hostel bed, several local meals, and enough left over for public transport and one or two modest paid attractions. You can further optimize your spending by taking advantage of lunch specials and free museum days which are common in many parts of Cyprus. For more Greek-influenced culture, see our overall guide to budget Greece.

The cost of food and transport in Cyprus is generally quite reasonable, particularly if you steer clear of the most tourist-heavy plazas. Local buses and trains offer a cost-effective way to get around, and street food vendors provide a delicious and cheap alternative to sit-down dining. By setting a daily limit and sticking to it, you can enjoy all the highlights of Cyprus without any financial stress during or after your trip.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Cyprus from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Cyprus is typically during April–June and September–November, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Cyprus cost from the UK?

Flights: £70–£200 return. Hotels: £40–£90 per night. Package holidays: £300–£600

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Cyprus?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Cyprus?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Cyprus holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Cyprus due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
