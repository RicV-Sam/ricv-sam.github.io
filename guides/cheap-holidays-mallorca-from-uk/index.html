Cheap Holidays To Mallorca From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Mallorca is one of the most popular and affordable holiday destinations for UK travellers, offering short flight times, reliable weather, and a wide range of accommodation options. From the vibrant capital of Palma to the famous resorts of Magaluf and the family-friendly beaches of Alcudia, there is something for every budget.

Due to strong package holiday competition and a high volume of low-cost flights from across Britain, it is often possible to find excellent value deals. Typical package prices range from £300–£550 per person, making it an accessible choice for those looking to enjoy the Mediterranean sun without overspending.

Cheapest Time To Visit Mallorca

The cheapest months to visit Mallorca from the UK are:

  • April–June
  • September–October

Travelling during these shoulder seasons provides significant price advantages, with lower hotel rates and more affordable flight options compared to the peak summer holidays.

Cheapest UK Airports

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£140 return
  • Hotels: £50–£110 per night
  • Package holidays: £300–£550

Budget Tips

  • Book 6–10 weeks early
  • Avoid peak summer
  • Use comparison sites
  • Travel midweek

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Mallorca from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Mallorca is typically during April–June and September–October, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Mallorca cost from the UK?

Flights: £40–£140 return. Hotels: £50–£110 per night. Package holidays: £300–£550

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Mallorca?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Mallorca?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Mallorca holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Mallorca due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
