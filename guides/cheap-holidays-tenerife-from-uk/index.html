Cheap Holidays To Tenerife From The UK

3 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Tenerife is the 'Island of Eternal Spring', dominated by the massive Mount Teide and featuring everything from lush pine forests to sun-baked southern beaches. For UK travellers, it's a reliable budget favourite that offers much more than just cheap resorts if you're willing to explore.

While the south is famous for its lively atmosphere, the north of the island offers a more authentic Canarian experience with stunning natural pools and historic towns. By utilizing the efficient 'TITSA' bus network and seeking out local 'guachinches' for food, you can experience the diverse microclimates of Tenerife on a very modest budget.

The cheapest months to visit Tenerife from the UK are:

  • May–June
  • September–November
  • January–March

Best Areas to Stay in Tenerife for Budget Travellers

In the north, **Puerto de la Cruz** is a fantastic, authentic base with great value hotels. In the south, **El Médano** is a cool, windsurfing town with a much more budget-friendly and local feel than the nearby tourist hubs. For those who want to be near the action, **Los Cristianos** often has better apartment deals than Playa de las Américas.

These months offer lower hotel prices and cheaper flights compared to peak school holiday periods.

Typical Daily Travel Costs in Tenerife

A daily budget of **£60 to **£100** will serve you well. This covers your stay, a 'Bono' bus card for travel, and plenty of local tapas and fresh fish. Mount Teide is free to visit (though the cable car has a fee), and the island's many stunning hikes cost nothing but your time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Tenerife from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Tenerife is typically during May–June, September–November and January–March, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Tenerife cost from the UK?

Flights: £60–£180 return. Hotels: £50–£120 per night. Package holidays: £350–£650

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Tenerife?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Tenerife?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Tenerife holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Tenerife due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
