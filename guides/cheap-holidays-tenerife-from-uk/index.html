Cheap Holidays To Tenerife From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Tenerife remains one of the most popular and affordable year-round destinations for UK travelers seeking sun without the long-haul price tag. Known for its diverse landscapes and year-round warm weather, the island offers everything from vibrant nightlife to relaxing coastal retreats. Major resorts like Costa Adeje, Playa de las Americas, and Los Cristianos are particularly well-serviced by budget airlines, making it easy to find competitive deals throughout the year.

Whether you are looking for a self-catering apartment or a full-board stay, Tenerife provides excellent value for money. Package holidays are a particularly cost-effective way to visit, with prices often ranging between £350–£650 per person including flights and accommodation. By choosing the right resort and timing your booking, you can enjoy a high-quality Canary Island escape on a modest budget.

Cheapest Time To Visit Tenerife

The cheapest months to visit Tenerife from the UK are:

  • May–June
  • September–November
  • January–March

These months offer lower hotel prices and cheaper flights compared to peak school holiday periods.

Cheapest UK Airports

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £60–£180 return
  • Hotels: £50–£120 per night
  • Package holidays: £350–£650

Budget Tips

  • Book 6–10 weeks early
  • Avoid school holidays
  • Use comparison sites
  • Travel midweek

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Tenerife from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Tenerife is typically during May–June, September–November and January–March, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Tenerife cost from the UK?

Flights: £60–£180 return. Hotels: £50–£120 per night. Package holidays: £350–£650

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Tenerife?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Tenerife?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Tenerife holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Tenerife due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
