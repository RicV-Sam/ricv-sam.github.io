Cheap Holidays To Singapore From The UK

1 min read

Singapore has a reputation for being expensive, but it's entirely possible to enjoy this futuristic city-state on a budget. By staying in neighborhoods like Little India or Geylang and eating at the legendary hawker centers, you can experience the best of Singapore for less.

Many of Singapore's most iconic sights, like the Gardens by the Bay light show and the stunning airport Jewel, are completely free to enjoy.

Cheapest Time To Visit Singapore

The cheapest months to visit Singapore from the UK are:

  • August to October
  • January to March (excluding Chinese New Year)

Avoiding major festivals and events like the F1 Grand Prix is essential for budget travel.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £550–£900 return
  • Hotels/Hostels: £30–£80 per night
  • Hawker meal: £4–£8

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Eat at Hawker Centers—the food is world-class and very cheap
  • Use the MRT (Subway)—it's clean, efficient, and very affordable
  • Visit free attractions like the Botanic Gardens and the Merlion Park
  • Drink tap water—it's safe and will save you a fortune over a week
