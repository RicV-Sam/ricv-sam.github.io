Cheap Holidays To Prague From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Prague is famous for being one of Europe's most affordable capitals, offering stunning Gothic architecture and a rich history at a fraction of the cost of Western cities. Only a 2-hour flight from the UK, it's perfect for a budget-friendly cultural escape.

Visitors can enjoy the Old Town and Charles Bridge without spending a penny, and the city's legendary beer remains some of the cheapest on the continent.

Cheapest Time To Visit Prague

The cheapest time to visit Prague is between January and March, or during the late autumn months of October and November.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Luton
  • Manchester
  • Edinburgh
  • Birmingham

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£120 return
  • Hotels: £60–£140 per night
  • City break packages: £180–£350

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Eat 'Lunch Specials' (polední menu) at local pubs
  • Avoid changing money at the airport or tourist stands
  • Walk the Royal Route for free sightseeing
  • Use the efficient tram network instead of taxis

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Prague from the UK?

January to March is the most affordable period, outside of the busy Christmas market season.

How much does a holiday to Prague cost from the UK?

Flights: £40–£120 return. Hotels: £60–£140 per night. City break packages: £180–£350

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Prague?

Václav Havel Airport (PRG) is well-connected to the UK via low-cost carriers like Ryanair and Wizz Air.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Prague?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance is recommended to find the best value on central accommodation.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

DIY booking is often cheaper for Prague due to the high volume of low-cost flights and varied hotel options.
