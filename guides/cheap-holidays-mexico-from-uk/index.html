Cheap Holidays To Mexico From The UK

1 min read

Mexico offers some of the world's best beaches, ancient ruins, and incredible food. For UK travellers, the Yucatan Peninsula (Cancun and Riviera Maya) is the main gateway, offering highly competitive all-inclusive deals that make long-haul travel affordable.

The direct flight from the UK is about 10–11 hours, and the sheer volume of tourists from Europe and North America keeps resort prices very competitive.

Cheapest Time To Visit Mexico

The cheapest months to visit Mexico from the UK are:

  • May and June
  • September to November

These months fall during the shoulder or hurricane season, where you can find the deepest discounts on luxury resorts.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Heathrow
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £450–£750 return
  • All-inclusive packages: £700–£1200
  • Tacos at a local stand: £1.50–£3

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Use the 'ADO' buses for safe, comfortable, and very cheap inter-city travel
  • Eat at local 'Taquerias' away from the main hotel zones
  • Visit public beaches like Playa Delfines for free instead of private beach clubs
  • Book excursions with local operators in town rather than through the hotel
