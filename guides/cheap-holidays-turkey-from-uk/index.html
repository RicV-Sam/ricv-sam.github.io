Cheap Holidays To Turkey From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Turkey is one of the cheapest long-haul feeling destinations available from the UK. With excellent weather, affordable hotels, and strong package holiday competition, Turkey offers exceptional value compared to many European destinations.

Popular destinations like Antalya, Dalaman, and Bodrum regularly offer full package holidays for under £400 per person when booked at the right time.

Cheapest Time To Visit Turkey

The cheapest months to visit Turkey from the UK are:

  • April to early June
  • September to November

These months offer lower hotel prices and cheaper flights compared to peak summer.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £60–£180 return
  • Hotels: £20–£50 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £300–£500

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Book 6–10 weeks before travel
  • Avoid peak July and August
  • Use price comparison websites
  • Travel midweek

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Turkey from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Turkey is typically during April to early June and September to November, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Turkey cost from the UK?

Flights: £60–£180 return. Hotels: £20–£50 per night. All-inclusive packages: £300–£500

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Turkey?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Turkey?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Turkey holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Turkey due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
