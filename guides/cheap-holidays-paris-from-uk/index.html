Cheap Holidays To Paris From The UK | Budget Travel Guide

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Paris continues to be one of the most visited cities in the world, and for British travellers, it is also one of the most accessible for a quick getaway. Whether you are looking for romantic weekend breaks or a family trip to see the iconic sights, the French capital offers an unparalleled mix of history, art, and gastronomy. While Paris has a reputation for being expensive, there are numerous ways to find budget hotels and affordable dining options, particularly in the arrondissements just outside the immediate city centre.

The proximity of Paris to the UK means there is intense competition between airlines and the Eurostar, helping to keep travel costs relatively low. Package holidays to Paris typically range from £150 to £350 per person, making it an excellent value choice for those seeking a sophisticated European experience on a budget. From exploring the charming streets of Montmartre to enjoying a picnic by the Seine, many of the city's best experiences are either free or very low cost.

Cheapest Time To Visit Paris

The cheapest months to visit Paris from the UK are:

  • January to March

The post-Christmas period and late winter months offer the lowest prices for accommodation and travel, as well as fewer crowds at major attractions.

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£120 return
  • Hotels: £70–£180 per night
  • Package holidays: £150–£350

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Use the RER train from Charles de Gaulle airport instead of a taxi
  • Look for "Bouillons"—traditional French restaurants that offer classic dishes at very low prices
  • Many major attractions are free for those under 26 who are EU residents (check current post-Brexit status for UK residents)
  • Enjoy free views of the city from the rooftop of Galeries Lafayette

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Paris from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Paris is typically during January to March, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Paris cost from the UK?

Flights: £40–£120 return. Hotels: £70–£180 per night. Package holidays: £150–£350

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Paris?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Paris?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Paris holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Paris due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
