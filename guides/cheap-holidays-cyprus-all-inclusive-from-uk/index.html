Cheap All Inclusive Holidays To Cyprus From The UK

1 min read

Cyprus is a leading destination for all-inclusive holidays, offering a high standard of resorts with excellent food and facilities. For UK travellers, an all-inclusive package in Cyprus can be a very cost-effective way to enjoy the Mediterranean sun without worrying about extra costs.

From the family-friendly resorts of Paphos to the lively hotels of Ayia Napa, all-inclusive deals frequently include flights, transfers, and all on-site dining.

Cheapest Time To Visit Cyprus

The cheapest months to visit Cyprus from the UK are:

  • November to March
  • April and May

Cyprus has one of the longest summer seasons in Europe, making it perfect for a cheap early or late-season sun break.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £50–£160 return
  • All-inclusive packages: £450–£800
  • Daily budget (if not all-in): £40–£70

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Book your all-inclusive package 6–9 months in advance for 'early bird' discounts
  • Look for resorts slightly outside the main town centers for better prices
  • Choose 'All-Inclusive Light' if you don't need unlimited alcoholic drinks
  • Use the local buses to visit free archaeological sites and beaches
