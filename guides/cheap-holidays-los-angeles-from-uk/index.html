Cheap Holidays To Los Angeles From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Los Angeles is a vast, exciting city that can be surprisingly budget-friendly. From the free Griffith Observatory views to the public beaches of Santa Monica and Venice, many of LA's most iconic experiences cost nothing.

The key to a cheap LA holiday is choosing your neighborhood carefully and using a mix of public transport and ride-shares to navigate the city's sprawl.

Cheapest Time To Visit Los Angeles

The cheapest months to visit Los Angeles from the UK are:

  • January to March
  • October and November

Winter in LA is mild and pleasant, with significantly lower hotel rates than the summer peak.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £350–£600 return
  • Hotels: £80–£150 per night
  • Taco Truck Meal: £8–£12

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Visit the Griffith Observatory for incredible free views of the Hollywood sign
  • Eat at the Grand Central Market for affordable and diverse food options
  • Use the Metro Rail system where possible to save on parking and Uber costs
  • Enjoy the free beaches at Santa Monica, Venice, and Malibu

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Los Angeles from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Los Angeles is typically during January to March and October and November, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Los Angeles cost from the UK?

Flights: £350–£600 return. Hotels: £80–£150 per night. Taco Truck Meal: £8–£12

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Los Angeles?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Los Angeles?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Los Angeles holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Los Angeles due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
