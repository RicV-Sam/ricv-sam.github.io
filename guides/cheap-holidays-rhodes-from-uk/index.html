Cheap Holidays To Rhodes From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Rhodes is one of the sunniest islands in Greece, making it a reliable choice for a budget holiday. With its impressive medieval Old Town, the stunning village of Lindos, and the lively resort of Faliraki, there is a huge range of accommodation to suit any budget.

The island's large size means there is plenty to see and do, and the strong competition between travel providers ensures prices remain low.

Cheapest Time To Visit Rhodes

The cheapest months to visit Rhodes from the UK are:

  • April to June
  • September to November

Rhodes stays warm late into the autumn, making it a great choice for a cheap late-season sun break.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Liverpool

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £60–£170 return
  • Hotels: £35–£75 per night
  • Entry to Rhodes Castle: £6

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Use the 'KTEL' buses to travel between Rhodes Town, Faliraki, and Lindos
  • Stay in self-catering studios in Faliraki for the best value rooms
  • Explore the Rhodes Old Town on foot—it's like an open-air museum for free
  • Look for 'Greek Nights' in smaller villages for affordable food and entertainment

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Rhodes from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Rhodes is typically during April to June and September to November, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Rhodes cost from the UK?

Flights: £60–£170 return. Hotels: £35–£75 per night. Entry to Rhodes Castle: £6

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Rhodes?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Rhodes?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Rhodes holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Rhodes due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
