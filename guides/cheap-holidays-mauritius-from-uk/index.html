Cheap Holidays To Mauritius From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Mauritius is a dream Indian Ocean destination that is more affordable than many realize. While famous for luxury resorts, the island also has a wide range of guesthouses and apartments, especially on the north and west coasts, that offer great value.

With its stunning beaches, lush mountains, and vibrant local markets, Mauritius offers a lot more than just sunbathing, and much of it can be enjoyed on a budget.

Cheapest Time To Visit Mauritius

The cheapest months to visit Mauritius from the UK are:

  • May to September

This is the Mauritian winter—the weather is still warm and sunny, but hotel prices are at their lowest and the island is less crowded.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £600–£950 return
  • Guesthouses/Apartments: £40–£90 per night
  • Street food 'Dholl Puri': £2

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Use the local bus network—it's cheap and covers the whole island
  • Eat local street food like 'Dholl Puri' and 'Gateaux Piment'
  • Stay in Flic en Flac or Grand Baie for the best range of budget options
  • All beaches are public—find your favorite spot and enjoy for free

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Mauritius from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Mauritius is typically during May to September, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Mauritius cost from the UK?

Flights: £600–£950 return. Guesthouses/Apartments: £40–£90 per night. Street food 'Dholl Puri': £2

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Mauritius?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Mauritius?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Mauritius holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Mauritius due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
