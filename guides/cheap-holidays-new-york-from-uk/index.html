Cheap Holidays To New York From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

New York City is the ultimate city break, and while it's known for being expensive, there are countless ways to see the Big Apple on a budget. From walking the High Line to taking the free Staten Island Ferry, the best of NYC often costs nothing.

Flying into JFK or Newark is generally the same price, so check both for the best deals, especially with low-cost carriers like Norse Atlantic.

Cheapest Time To Visit New York

The cheapest months to visit New York from the UK are:

  • January to March
  • September and October (shoulder)

Early spring is particularly affordable as hotel rates drop significantly after the holiday season.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Heathrow
  • Manchester

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £300–£550 return
  • Hotels (Queens/Brooklyn): £80–£150 per night
  • Slice of Pizza: £1–£3

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Stay in Long Island City (Queens) or Brooklyn for much better hotel value
  • Eat 'Street Food' (hot dogs, pretzels, halal carts) for cheap lunches
  • Take the Staten Island Ferry for a free view of the Statue of Liberty
  • Use the Subway—it's the fastest and cheapest way to get around

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit New York from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit New York is typically during January to March and September and October (shoulder), when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to New York cost from the UK?

Flights: £300–£550 return. Hotels (Queens/Brooklyn): £80–£150 per night. Slice of Pizza: £1–£3

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to New York?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to New York?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for New York holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to New York due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
