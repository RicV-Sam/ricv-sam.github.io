Cheap Holidays To Corfu From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Corfu is one of Greece's greenest and most popular islands, offering a huge range of budget-friendly options. From the party atmosphere of Kavos to the family-friendly beaches of Sidari and the stunning architecture of Corfu Town, there's something for everyone.

The island's proximity to the UK means there are frequent low-cost flights, especially from regional airports, making it a very accessible budget destination.

Cheapest Time To Visit Corfu

The cheapest months to visit Corfu from the UK are:

  • May and June
  • September and October

The shoulder season offers warm weather and clear skies, but without the peak-summer crowds or prices.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • East Midlands
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £50–£140 return
  • Hotels: £35–£70 per night
  • Gyro (local wrap): £3.50

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Eat 'Gyros' for a delicious, filling, and very cheap meal
  • Use the 'Green Buses' for affordable travel across the whole island
  • Stay in smaller villages slightly inland for much lower room rates
  • Take advantage of free beaches—most are easily accessible

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Corfu from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Corfu is typically during May and June and September and October, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Corfu cost from the UK?

Flights: £50–£140 return. Hotels: £35–£70 per night. Gyro (local wrap): £3.50

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Corfu?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Corfu?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Corfu holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Corfu due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
