Cheap Holidays To Canary Islands From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

The Canary Islands are the UK's go-to for year-round sun. With four main islands to choose from—Tenerife, Gran Canaria, Lanzarote, and Fuerteventura—there are endless options for budget-friendly beach breaks, volcanic hiking, and water sports.

Because there are so many flights from almost every UK airport, the competition keeps prices low even during the winter months.

Cheapest Time To Visit Canary Islands

The cheapest months to visit the Canary Islands from the UK are:

  • January and February (excluding half-term)
  • May and June
  • November

Avoiding school holidays is the key to finding the lowest prices for these popular islands.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Luton
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • East Midlands
  • Glasgow

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £50–£150 return
  • Hotels: £40–£80 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £400–£750

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Duty-free status means alcohol, tobacco, and perfume are very cheap
  • Rent a car to explore the interior—it's often cheaper than organized tours
  • Eat at 'Guachinches' in Tenerife for authentic and cheap local food
  • Use the reliable 'TITSA' bus network in Tenerife or 'Global' in Gran Canaria

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Canary Islands from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Canary Islands is typically during January and February (excluding half-term), May and June and November, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Canary Islands cost from the UK?

Flights: £50–£150 return. Hotels: £40–£80 per night. All-inclusive packages: £400–£750

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Canary Islands?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Canary Islands?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Canary Islands holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Canary Islands due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
