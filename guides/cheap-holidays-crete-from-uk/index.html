Cheap Holidays To Crete From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Crete is a versatile destination that offers everything from ancient ruins to some of the world's best beaches. As Greece's largest island, it has a wide range of accommodation options that cater to every budget, all within a 4-hour flight from the UK.

Whether you're exploring the Venetian harbour of Chania or relaxing on the pink sands of Elafonisi, Crete provides a high-quality holiday experience at a very reasonable price point.

Cheapest Time To Visit Crete

The cheapest months to visit Crete are May and October, during the shoulder season when the weather is still pleasant.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £80–£190 return
  • Hotels: £50–£120 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £350–£600

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Eat at local tavernas away from the main tourist strips
  • Rent a small car to explore hidden, free beaches
  • Travel outside of school holidays for the best deals
  • Buy local produce and wine from supermarkets

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Crete from the UK?

May and October offer the best combination of warm weather and low prices.

How much does a holiday to Crete cost from the UK?

Flights: £80–£190 return. Hotels: £50–£120 per night. All-inclusive packages: £350–£600

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Crete?

Heraklion (HER) and Chania (CHQ) are the two main gateways, both served by major UK low-cost carriers.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Crete?

Booking 3–4 months in advance is recommended for peak season, but shoulder season deals can be found later.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Packages often provide the best value for Crete, especially when they include transfers and meals.
