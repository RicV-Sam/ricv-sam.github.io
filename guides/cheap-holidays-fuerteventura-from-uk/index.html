Cheap Holidays To Fuerteventura From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Fuerteventura is the second largest of the Canary Islands and is famous for its vast sand dunes and world-class surfing beaches. At 4.5 hours from the UK, it's a perfect year-round escape for those on a budget.

Whether you're exploring the lively town of Corralejo or the quieter southern beaches, the island's wide range of accommodation ensures there's something for every price point.

Cheapest Time To Visit Fuerteventura

The most affordable months to visit Fuerteventura are January to March and the month of November.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £70–£180 return
  • Hotels: £60–£130 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £350–£600

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Explore the Corralejo Dunes for free
  • Eat fresh local goat cheese from village markets
  • Travel midweek to secure the lowest flight fares
  • Visit the historic village of Betancuria

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Fuerteventura from the UK?

January to March is the best time for a budget-friendly winter sun break.

How much does a holiday to Fuerteventura cost from the UK?

Flights: £70–£180 return. Hotels: £60–£130 per night. All-inclusive packages: £350–£600

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Fuerteventura?

El Matorral Airport (FUE) receives direct low-cost flights from most major UK airports.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Fuerteventura?

Booking 8–12 weeks in advance is recommended to find the best value on resort stays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Packages are often the most cost-effective way to visit Fuerteventura, especially for family holidays.
