Cheap Holidays To Bulgaria From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Bulgaria has long been a favorite for budget-conscious UK travellers. Sunny Beach on the Black Sea coast is famous for its low-cost nightlife and affordable hotels, while the mountains offer some of Europe's cheapest skiing in Bansko and Borovets.

The cost of living in Bulgaria is significantly lower than in the UK, meaning your spending money goes much further on food, drinks, and activities.

Cheapest Time To Visit Bulgaria

The cheapest months to visit Bulgaria from the UK are:

  • June and September (Beach)
  • January and March (Ski)

Avoid the peak school holiday weeks in August and February for the best deals.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Luton
  • Manchester
  • Newcastle

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £50–£140 return
  • Hotels: £20–£45 per night
  • 3-course meal: £10–£15

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Look for all-inclusive packages which are exceptionally cheap here
  • Use the local buses to travel between Burgas, Sunny Beach, and Nessebar
  • Drink local Bulgarian wine and beer for the best value
  • Book ski hire and lift passes in advance online

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Bulgaria from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Bulgaria is typically during June and September (Beach) and January and March (Ski), when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Bulgaria cost from the UK?

Flights: £50–£140 return. Hotels: £20–£45 per night. 3-course meal: £10–£15

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Bulgaria?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Bulgaria?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Bulgaria holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Bulgaria due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
