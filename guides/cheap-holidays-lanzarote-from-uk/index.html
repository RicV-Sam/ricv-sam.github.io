Cheap Holidays To Lanzarote From The UK

3 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

For many UK holidaymakers, Lanzarote represents the perfect blend of exotic allure and wallet-friendly practicality. If you enjoy volcanic landscapes, you might also like budget travel in Fuerteventura. From the moment you arrive to see the volcanic Timanfaya National Park or wander around the unique Jameos del Agua, you'll realize why this has become such a popular choice for those looking to maximize their travel budget. With frequent flights and diverse package options departing from regional UK airports, the journey to Lanzarote is both convenient and surprisingly economical.

What truly sets Lanzarote apart is the wealth of low-cost activities and dining options available. You can spend your days visiting the Papagayo beaches or simply soaking in the local atmosphere, knowing that your money goes much further here than in typical European capitals. By prioritizing striking volcanic landscapes and César Manrique's art and opting for authentic local experiences, you can craft a memorable itinerary that respects your financial boundaries without sacrificing any of the magic.

Cheapest Time To Visit Lanzarote

The cheapest months for Lanzarote are typically January to March, and late autumn in November.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • East Midlands

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £70–£190 return
  • Hotels: £60–£130 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £350–£600

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Eat at 'Teleclubs'—these are local community centres that serve some of the best and cheapest food on the island.
  • Rent a car; Lanzarote is small and easy to drive, and it's the only way to see the best volcanic sites at your own pace.
  • The local wine from the La Geria region is unique and very affordable when bought in local shops.
  • Visit the Timanfaya National Park early in the morning to avoid the tour bus queues.

Best Areas to Stay in Lanzarote for Budget Travellers

**Puerto del Carmen** is the main hub and offers the widest variety of budget accommodation and cheap eats. For a more tranquil and wind-swept experience, **Costa Teguise** provides excellent value, while **Arrecife** (the capital) is the best place to stay for a truly local, non-touristy vibe with great transport links.

Typical Daily Travel Costs in Lanzarote

Expect to spend **£60 to £95 per day**. This includes a comfortable self-catering apartment, a small rental car to explore the island's volcanic sites, and dining at local 'Teleclubs' (social clubs) which serve amazing, cheap Canarian food. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Lanzarote from the UK?

January through March offers the best value for those seeking winter sun without the high price tag.

How much does a holiday to Lanzarote cost from the UK?

Flights: £70–£190 return. Hotels: £60–£130 per night. All-inclusive packages: £350–£600

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Lanzarote?

Arrecife Airport (ACE) is the island's hub, with frequent budget flights from the UK.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Lanzarote?

Booking 2–3 months in advance usually secures a good deal on both flights and accommodation.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays are extremely competitive in Lanzarote and often provide the best value.
