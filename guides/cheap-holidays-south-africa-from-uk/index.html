Cheap Holidays To South Africa From The UK

1 min read

South Africa offers an incredible diversity of experiences, from the iconic Table Mountain in Cape Town to world-class safaris and stunning coastlines. For UK travellers, the favorable exchange rate often makes South Africa an exceptionally high-value destination.

Whether you're tasting wine in Stellenbosch or searching for the Big Five in Kruger, your money goes a long way once you're on the ground.

Cheapest Time To Visit South Africa

The cheapest months to visit South Africa from the UK are:

  • May to September

This is the South African winter. It's the best time for game viewing in the north and offers the lowest prices for accommodation in Cape Town.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £550–£900 return
  • Hotels/Guesthouses: £40–£90 per night
  • Bottle of good local wine: £5–£10

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • The exchange rate is your friend—luxury experiences are often very affordable
  • Use the 'MyCiTi' bus in Cape Town or Uber for safe and cheap transport
  • Look for 'SanParks' accommodation for the most affordable safari stays
  • Eat at local markets like the Neighbourgoods Market for great value food
