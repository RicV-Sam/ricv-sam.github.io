Cheap Holidays To Tunisia From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Tunisia offers beautiful Mediterranean beaches, impressive Roman ruins, and Sahara desert adventures at very competitive prices. It is particularly well-known for high-quality, low-cost all-inclusive resorts that are perfect for families and couples alike.

The flight time is approximately 3 hours, making it a quick and affordable escape to a completely different culture.

Cheapest Time To Visit Tunisia

The cheapest months to visit Tunisia from the UK are:

  • October to April
  • May and June

Spring and autumn offer perfect weather for sightseeing without the intense heat of mid-summer.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £150–£300 return
  • Hotels: £25–£55 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £350–£600

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • All-inclusive deals often represent the best value here
  • Use 'Louages' (shared taxis) for cheap transport between towns
  • Visit the local Medinas for affordable leather goods and spices
  • Explore free or low-cost sites like the amphitheatres and ruins

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Tunisia from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Tunisia is typically during October to April and May and June, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Tunisia cost from the UK?

Flights: £150–£300 return. Hotels: £25–£55 per night. All-inclusive packages: £350–£600

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Tunisia?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Tunisia?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Tunisia holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Tunisia due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
