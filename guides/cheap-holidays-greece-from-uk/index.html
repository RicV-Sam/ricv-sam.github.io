Cheap Holidays To Greece From The UK | Budget Travel Guide

1 min read

Greece remains one of the most beloved summer destinations for British travellers, offering an intoxicating blend of ancient history, sun-drenched islands, and world-class hospitality. From the sprawling olive groves of Crete to the iconic sunset views in Santorini, the variety of experiences is vast. Despite its enduring popularity, Greece remains remarkably affordable for those who know where to look, especially when compared to other Mediterranean hotspots.

The high level of airline competition from major UK hubs like London and Manchester helps keep flight prices low, particularly during the shoulder seasons. Budget-conscious travellers can often find excellent value in package holidays, with typical prices ranging from £350 to £650 per person. Whether you are seeking the vibrant nightlife of Corfu or the historic charm of Rhodes, there are numerous ways to enjoy a Greek getaway without overspending.

Cheapest Time To Visit Greece

The cheapest months to visit Greece from the UK are:

  • May to June
  • September to October

These shoulder season months offer much better value for hotels and flights while still providing excellent weather for exploring the islands.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £80–£220 return
  • Hotels: £50–£120 per night
  • Package holidays: £350–£650

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Book during the shoulder seasons to avoid peak summer prices
  • Look for flights into larger hubs like Athens or Heraklion
  • Use local ferries instead of domestic flights for island hopping
  • Eat at local tavernas away from the main tourist strips
