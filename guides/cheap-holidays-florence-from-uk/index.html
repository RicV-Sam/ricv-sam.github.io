Cheap Holidays To Florence From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Florence is the jewel of Tuscany and a must-visit for art lovers. With a flight time of around 2 hours and 15 minutes, it's a manageable and rewarding destination for a budget-conscious traveller.

The city is essentially an open-air museum, where you can see incredible architecture and sculptures just by walking the streets. Exploring the Oltrarno area across the river provides a glimpse into local life with more affordable prices than the area around the Duomo.

Cheapest Time To Visit Florence

January, February, and November are the most affordable months for a Florence city break.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • East Midlands

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £55–£160 return
  • Hotels: £75–£170 per night
  • City break packages: £280–£450

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Visit the free-to-enter churches for stunning art
  • Eat panini from famous stalls like All'Antico Vinaio
  • Watch the sunset from Piazzale Michelangelo for the best free view
  • Consider flying to Pisa and taking the cheap train to Florence

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Florence from the UK?

The winter months of January and February offer the best value for flights and hotels.

How much does a holiday to Florence cost from the UK?

Flights: £55–£160 return. Hotels: £75–£170 per night. City break packages: £280–£450

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Florence?

Florence Airport (FLR) is convenient, but many budget travellers fly into Pisa (PSA) due to lower fares.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Florence?

Booking 10 weeks in advance usually yields the best balance of price and choice.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

DIY booking is often preferred as it allows for flexibility in choosing flights from Pisa or Florence.
