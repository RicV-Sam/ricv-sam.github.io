Cheap Holidays To Morocco From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Morocco is a vibrant, exotic destination just a few hours from the UK. Whether you're getting lost in the souks of Marrakech, surfing in Taghazout, or relaxing in Agadir, Morocco offers a unique cultural experience that is highly affordable.

Staying in a traditional Riad (guesthouse) often provides a more authentic and better-value experience than large western-style hotels.

Cheapest Time To Visit Morocco

The cheapest months to visit Morocco from the UK are:

  • November to February
  • May and June

Winter is mild and perfect for exploring cities, while the coast remains pleasant year-round.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£130 return
  • Riads: £30–£60 per night
  • Street food Tagine: £4–£7

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Stay in a Riad inside the Medina for better prices and atmosphere
  • Drink mint tea—it's delicious, traditional, and very cheap
  • Haggling is expected in markets; never pay the first price
  • Use the CTM or Supratours buses for reliable and cheap inter-city travel

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Morocco from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Morocco is typically during November to February and May and June, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Morocco cost from the UK?

Flights: £40–£130 return. Riads: £30–£60 per night. Street food Tagine: £4–£7

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Morocco?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Morocco?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Morocco holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Morocco due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
