Cheap All Inclusive Holidays To Portugal From The UK

1 min read

Portugal, particularly the Algarve region, is a fantastic choice for an all-inclusive holiday. Known for its stunning cliffs, Atlantic beaches, and world-class golf courses, Portugal offers a slightly different vibe to the Mediterranean resorts while maintaining excellent value.

Albufeira and Portimão have a great range of all-inclusive hotels that are perfect for families and groups looking for a predictable holiday cost.

Cheapest Time To Visit Portugal

The cheapest months to visit Portugal from the UK are:

  • November to March
  • April and May

The Algarve is a great winter destination for walking and golf, with very low all-inclusive rates during the off-season.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Bristol
  • Leeds Bradford

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£130 return
  • All-inclusive packages: £400–£750
  • Coffee (if not all-in): £1

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Look for resorts near Albufeira for the most competitive pricing
  • Fly into Faro and use a pre-booked shuttle for the cheapest transfers
  • Check out 'All-Inclusive' deals in Madeira for a different, lush island experience
  • Use the 'Comboios de Portugal' (train) for cheap travel along the coast
