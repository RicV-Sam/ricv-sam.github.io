Cheap Holidays To Dubrovnik From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Dubrovnik, with its stunning stone walls and azure waters, is one of Europe's most beautiful destinations. Accessible via a 2.5-hour flight from the UK, it can be expensive, but careful planning makes it reachable for budget travellers.

By staying just outside the Old Town in areas like Lapad and using public transport, you can enjoy everything the 'Pearl of the Adriatic' has to offer without the premium price tag.

Cheapest Time To Visit Dubrovnik

April, May, and October are the most affordable months to visit Dubrovnik.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Edinburgh

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £80–£200 return
  • Hotels: £70–£150 per night
  • Holiday packages: £450–£650

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Buy the Dubrovnik Pass for entry to the walls and museums
  • Stay in Lapad or Babin Kuk for better value accommodation
  • Take the local bus from the airport instead of a taxi
  • Enjoy a picnic on the rocks at Danče beach

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Dubrovnik from the UK?

April and October are the cheapest months, offering a balance of mild weather and lower costs.

How much does a holiday to Dubrovnik cost from the UK?

Flights: £80–£200 return. Hotels: £70–£150 per night. Holiday packages: £450–£650

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Dubrovnik?

Dubrovnik Airport (DBV) is served by several low-cost carriers from across the UK.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Dubrovnik?

Booking at least 4 months in advance is vital for finding budget-friendly stays in this popular city.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Packages can often be cheaper for Dubrovnik, as they may include hotels that are difficult to book affordably on their own.
