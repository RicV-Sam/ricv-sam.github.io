Cheap Holidays To Egypt From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Egypt is a fantastic year-round destination offering incredible value for UK sun-seekers. Whether you're looking for world-class diving in the Red Sea at Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh or exploring ancient history in Cairo and Luxor, Egypt delivers high quality for low prices.

Flight times are manageable at around 5 hours, making it a popular alternative to the Canary Islands for winter sun.

Cheapest Time To Visit Egypt

The cheapest months to visit Egypt from the UK are:

  • January to March
  • June and July (due to high heat)

Winter offers the best balance of price and comfortable weather for sightseeing.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Luton
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £200–£400 return
  • Hotels: £25–£60 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £400–£700

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Look for all-inclusive deals which are very common and high value
  • Use local ride-hailing apps like Uber or Careem in cities
  • Haggle respectfully in souks and markets
  • Drink bottled water only (cheap and essential)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Egypt from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Egypt is typically during January to March and June and July (due to high heat), when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Egypt cost from the UK?

Flights: £200–£400 return. Hotels: £25–£60 per night. All-inclusive packages: £400–£700

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Egypt?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Egypt?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Egypt holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Egypt due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
