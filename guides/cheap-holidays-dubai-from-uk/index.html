Cheap Holidays To Dubai From The UK | Budget Travel Guide

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Dubai has successfully transformed itself from a niche luxury destination into a global travel hub that is surprisingly accessible for UK holidaymakers. While it is famous for its record-breaking skyscrapers and high-end shopping, there are plenty of ways to experience the city on a budget. By timing your visit correctly and choosing your location wisely—such as staying near Dubai Marina or the historic Old Dubai—you can enjoy the sunshine and glamour without the premium price tag.

The sheer volume of flights from major UK airports, including direct services from London, Birmingham, and Glasgow, has increased airline competition and brought down fares. Package holidays to Dubai now typically range from £500 to £900 per person, offering excellent value for a long-haul destination. From the world-class attractions of Downtown Dubai to the luxury of the Palm Jumeirah, the city offers a unique blend of modern marvels and traditional charm.

Cheapest Time To Visit Dubai

The cheapest months to visit Dubai from the UK are:

  • May
  • June
  • September

Visiting during these hotter months can result in significant savings on luxury hotels, though it is best to avoid the peak December period when prices are at their highest.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Glasgow

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £350–£650 return
  • Hotels: £80–£180 per night
  • Package holidays: £500–£900

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Use the Dubai Metro to travel between major attractions cheaply
  • Eat in Deira or Bur Dubai for authentic and affordable local cuisine
  • Visit public beaches like JBR or Kite Beach instead of private hotel clubs
  • Book your Burj Khalifa tickets in advance to secure the best rates

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Dubai from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Dubai is typically during May, June and September, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Dubai cost from the UK?

Flights: £350–£650 return. Hotels: £80–£180 per night. Package holidays: £500–£900

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Dubai?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Dubai?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Dubai holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Dubai due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
