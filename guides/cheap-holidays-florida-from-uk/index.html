Cheap Holidays To Florida From The UK

1 min read

Florida, the Sunshine State, is a dream destination for many UK families. While theme parks in Orlando can be expensive, there are many ways to manage costs, from choosing off-site accommodation to visiting free natural springs and beaches.

The huge volume of flights from the UK to Orlando and Miami means that prices are often lower than other US destinations.

Cheapest Time To Visit Florida

The cheapest months to visit Florida from the UK are:

  • January and February
  • September and October
  • Early November

Avoiding the US and UK school holidays is vital for both lower flight prices and shorter theme park queues.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick (to Orlando MCO)
  • London Heathrow (to Miami)
  • Manchester

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £350–£650 return
  • Budget Hotels/Villas: £60–£120 per night
  • Theme park ticket (1 day): £100+

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Stay in a villa or off-site hotel with a kitchen to save on food costs
  • Buy multi-day theme park passes in advance from UK-based brokers
  • Visit free attractions like Disney Springs or local state parks
  • Rent a car—it's essential and often very affordable if booked early
