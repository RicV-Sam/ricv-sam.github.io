Cheap Holidays To Algarve From The UK

3 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

The Algarve from the UK is the definitive sun-and-sea escape, offering some of the best coastline in Europe at prices that are remarkably friendly to the British pound. From the dramatic golden cliffs of Lagos to the authentic historic charm of Tavira, the Algarve provides a diverse holiday experience that respects a modest budget. If you enjoy Portuguese culture, you might also be interested in our wider guide to budget Portugal. For UK travellers, the sheer volume of budget flights to Faro makes it a top-tier candidate for a 2026 value sun break.

The secret to a cheap Algarve trip is exploring beyond the main tourist marinas. Spend your days discovering hidden coves or hiking the scenic Seven Hanging Valleys Trail for free. By staying in value-focused hubs like Portimão or the historic center of Lagos and dining on the "Prato do Dia" (Plate of the Day), you can experience the legendary Portuguese hospitality without needing a luxury budget. From the world-class surf of the west coast to the tranquil islands of the Ria Formosa, the Algarve offers a depth of beauty that is as accessible as it is unforgettable.

Cheapest Time To Visit Algarve

January to March and the months of October and November are the cheapest times to visit the Algarve.

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £45–£145 return from major UK airports
  • Hotels: £45–£115 per night for a well-located guesthouse or apartment
  • All-inclusive packages: £280–£520 for a 7-night sun break

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Eat the 'Prato do Dia': Follow the locals at lunchtime. Most traditional Portuguese restaurants offer a "Plate of the Day" for €8-€12, which usually includes a main dish, drink, and coffee.
  • Use the Regional Train: Don't spend a fortune on private transfers. The regional train line (Linha do Algarve) connects Faro to Lagos and Tavira for just a few Euros, offering a scenic and cheap way to travel.
  • Shop at Pingo Doce: This local supermarket chain is excellent for fresh produce and high-quality Portuguese wine at incredibly low prices. It's the best way to stock up for a budget beach picnic.
  • Visit the Municipal Markets: For the freshest fish and local fruit, head to the "Mercado Municipal" in towns like Olhão or Loulé. They are free to explore and offer genuine local prices.

Best Areas to Stay in Algarve for Budget Travellers

To find the best value and avoid the highest prices of the luxury golf resorts, consider these locations:

  • Portimão: A larger port city that offers much better value for money on accommodation and dining than nearby Lagos, while still being minutes from the stunning Praia da Rocha.
  • Lagos (Old Town): While popular, staying in the guesthouses within the historic walls is often cheaper than the modern marina-side developments and much more authentic.
  • Quarteira: A more local, high-rise resort area that offers some of the most competitive apartment prices in the central Algarve, with a great beach and local market.

Typical Daily Travel Costs in Algarve

A daily budget of **£55 to £90** is a good target. This covers a nice apartment or guesthouse, a rental car (highly recommended for exploring), and plenty of fresh seafood and local wine. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Algarve from the UK?

January through March is the most affordable period, ideal for walking and exploring.

How much does a holiday to Algarve cost from the UK?

Flights: £50–£160 return. Hotels: £50–£130 per night. All-inclusive packages: £300–£550

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Algarve?

Faro Airport (FAO) is the primary gateway, with low-cost flights from across the UK.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Algarve?

Booking 2 months in advance is generally sufficient for finding great value deals.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays are excellent for the Algarve's resort towns and often include great perks.
