Cheap Holidays To Budapest From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Budapest is frequently cited as one of Europe's best-value cities. Only 2.5 hours from the UK, the Hungarian capital offers a stunning mix of history, architecture, and a unique nightlife scene.

From the thermal baths to the famous ruin bars, Budapest provides a wealth of experiences that won't break the bank. Exploring both the Buda and Pest sides of the river on foot or via the excellent tram system is both easy and affordable.

Cheapest Time To Visit Budapest

The cheapest months to visit Budapest are January to March and the month of November.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Luton
  • Manchester
  • Edinburgh
  • Birmingham

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£130 return
  • Hotels: £60–£140 per night
  • City break packages: £190–£380

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Buy a 24-hour or 72-hour travel card for unlimited transport
  • Visit the public thermal baths midweek for lower prices
  • Enjoy the free views from Fisherman's Bastion and Gellért Hill
  • Eat 'Lángos' or visit the Great Market Hall for cheap eats

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Budapest from the UK?

January to March is the most affordable period for a visit to the Hungarian capital.

How much does a holiday to Budapest cost from the UK?

Flights: £40–£130 return. Hotels: £60–£140 per night. City break packages: £190–£380

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Budapest?

Budapest Ferenc Liszt International (BUD) is well-served by Wizz Air and Ryanair from across the UK.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Budapest?

Booking 6–8 weeks in advance is ideal for securing the best rates on central apartments.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

DIY booking is incredibly popular for Budapest due to the abundance of cheap flights and affordable private rentals.
