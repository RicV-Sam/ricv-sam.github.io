Cheap Holidays To Cape Verde From The UK

3 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Exploring Cape Verde on a budget is not only possible but incredibly rewarding for savvy travellers from the UK. Whether your heart is set on witnessing the salt mines of Pedra de Lume, exploring the depths of the vibrant markets of Mindelo, or experiencing the endless dunes of Boa Vista, you'll find that this destination caters exceptionally well to the cost-conscious. The accessibility provided by a wide range of airline partners means that finding a deal to Cape Verde is often just a matter of timing and a bit of research.

Once you've touched down, Cape Verde reveals its true value through its affordable infrastructure and local charm. The availability of a unique blend of African and Portuguese cultures ensures that every day is filled with wonder, regardless of your daily spend. By engaging with the local culture and choosing to stay in traditional neighbourhoods, you'll gain a deeper appreciation for Cape Verde while enjoying a holiday that is as affordable as it is unforgettable.

Cheapest Time To Visit Cape Verde

The cheapest months to visit Cape Verde from the UK are:

  • May and June
  • September and October

The weather is warm year-round, but these shoulder months often see a drop in package prices.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Bristol

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £300–£550 return
  • All-inclusive packages: £600–£900
  • Dinner at a local restaurant: £15–£25

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • All-inclusive packages are often the most cost-effective way to visit
  • Eat 'Catchupa' (the national dish)—it's delicious, filling, and cheap
  • Stay in Santa Maria (Sal) for the most budget-friendly dining and activity options
  • Check TUI for last-minute charter flight deals to Sal or Boa Vista

Best Areas to Stay in Cape Verde for Budget Travellers

Finding the perfect base in Cape Verde doesn't have to break the bank if you know where to look. For the best deals, focus your search on up-and-coming neighbourhoods like Santa Maria in Sal, which offer a great selection of budget accommodation. These spots are typically just a short hop from the main tourist zones, providing the perfect balance between convenience and cost-effectiveness.

Furthermore, districts like the cultural hub of Mindelo and Sal Rei in Boa Vista are renowned for their student populations and local markets, which translates to affordable beds and even cheaper food. By choosing to stay in these lively, authentic areas, you'll benefit from a more immersive experience in Cape Verde while keeping your accommodation costs remarkably low. It's an excellent strategy for anyone looking to see the best of Cape Verde on a strict budget.

Typical Daily Travel Costs in Cape Verde

To enjoy Cape Verde without overspending, a daily budget of approximately £50 and £80 is usually sufficient for the average backpacker or budget tourist. This figure accounts for basic accommodation, local transport, and satisfying meals from local eateries. It's a great destination for those who want a rich cultural experience without the high price tags found in other global hotspots, provided you make smart choices about where you eat and how you move around.

Transport costs in Cape Verde are often one of the lowest parts of a traveller's budget, thanks to an extensive network of affordable local options. Similarly, food costs can be kept low by visiting local markets and bakeries for breakfast and lunch. By allocating your funds wisely and prioritizing the sights that matter most to you, you'll find that Cape Verde offers incredible value for every pound spent.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Cape Verde from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Cape Verde is typically during May and June and September and October, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Cape Verde cost from the UK?

Flights: £300–£550 return. All-inclusive packages: £600–£900. Dinner at a local restaurant: £15–£25

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Cape Verde?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Cape Verde?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Cape Verde holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Cape Verde due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
