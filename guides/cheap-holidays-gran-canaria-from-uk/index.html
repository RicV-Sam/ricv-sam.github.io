Cheap Holidays To Gran Canaria From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Gran Canaria is often described as a 'mini-continent' due to its diverse landscapes, from the dunes of Maspalomas to the lush green mountains of the north. A 4.5-hour flight from the UK brings you to this year-round budget favourite.

By exploring the capital, Las Palmas, or the smaller mountain villages, travellers can find a more local and affordable side to the island away from the main tourist hubs.

Cheapest Time To Visit Gran Canaria

January, February, and the shoulder months of May and June are typically the cheapest for Gran Canaria.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • East Midlands

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £80–£200 return
  • Hotels: £60–£140 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £380–£650

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Use the extensive and cheap 'Global' bus network
  • Visit Las Palmas for more affordable, local dining options
  • Enjoy the free trails and hikes in the central mountains
  • Spend a day at the Maspalomas Dunes for free

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Gran Canaria from the UK?

January and February offer the lowest prices for those seeking a warm winter escape.

How much does a holiday to Gran Canaria cost from the UK?

Flights: £80–£200 return. Hotels: £60–£140 per night. All-inclusive packages: £380–£650

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Gran Canaria?

Gran Canaria Airport (LPA) is a major hub with numerous budget connections from the UK.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Gran Canaria?

Booking 3 months in advance is a good rule of thumb for securing affordable resort accommodation.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays are highly recommended for Gran Canaria to get the best overall value on flights and hotels.
