Cheap Holidays To Dominican Republic From The UK

1 min read

The Dominican Republic is often the most affordable Caribbean destination for UK travellers. Famous for its white sand beaches and extensive all-inclusive resorts, it provides a luxury tropical experience at a much lower price point than neighbouring islands.

Punta Cana is the main resort area, while Puerto Plata in the north often offers even better value for those on a strict budget.

Cheapest Time To Visit Dominican Republic

The cheapest months to visit the Dominican Republic from the UK are:

  • May and June
  • September to November

Avoiding the Christmas and New Year peak is essential for securing a budget deal.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £450–£700 return
  • All-inclusive packages: £650–£1000
  • Local beer (Presidente): £2–£4

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • All-inclusive is almost always the cheapest way to experience the DR
  • Use 'Guaguas' (local buses) for a very cheap, if adventurous, way to get around
  • Look for last-minute deals from charter companies like TUI
  • Stick to the resort for most meals to maximize your all-inclusive value
