Cheap Holidays To Malaysia From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Malaysia is a fascinating mix of modern cities, ancient rainforests, and stunning islands. It is one of the most developed countries in Southeast Asia, yet it remains very affordable for UK travellers, especially when it comes to world-class food.

Kuala Lumpur is a great hub for budget travel, while the islands of Penang (for food) and Langkawi (duty-free) offer excellent value beach breaks.

Cheapest Time To Visit Malaysia

The cheapest months to visit Malaysia from the UK are:

  • March to May
  • September to November

These shoulder periods offer a good balance of weather and price across both the east and west coasts.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £550–£850 return
  • Hotels: £20–£50 per night
  • Hawker Center Meal: £3–£6

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Eat at 'Hawker Centers' for some of the world's best and cheapest food
  • Use the excellent and cheap bus network for inter-city travel
  • Visit the many free attractions in KL, such as the Batu Caves
  • Langkawi is a duty-free island, making it the cheapest place for drinks

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Malaysia from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Malaysia is typically during March to May and September to November, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Malaysia cost from the UK?

Flights: £550–£850 return. Hotels: £20–£50 per night. Hawker Center Meal: £3–£6

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Malaysia?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Malaysia?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Malaysia holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Malaysia due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
