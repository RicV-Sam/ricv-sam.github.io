Cheap Holidays To Cambodia From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Cambodia is a captivating destination that remains one of the most affordable in Southeast Asia. Home to the legendary Angkor Wat, it offers a deep cultural history alongside beautiful islands and a low cost of living that is perfect for UK budget travellers.

While most visitors come for the temples of Siem Reap, the capital Phnom Penh and the southern islands like Koh Rong offer great value and unique experiences.

Cheapest Time To Visit Cambodia

The cheapest months to visit Cambodia from the UK are:

  • May to September

The wet season brings lush green landscapes and the lowest prices for accommodation, often with 50% discounts on luxury hotels.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick

You'll usually fly into Phnom Penh or Siem Reap with a stopover in Bangkok or Singapore.

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £600–£900 return
  • Hotels: £10–£30 per night
  • Beer (Angkor): £0.50–£1

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Rent a bicycle to explore Siem Reap and the outskirts
  • Stay in 'Boutique Hostels' which offer hotel-quality rooms at hostel prices
  • Eat at local markets for the best value food
  • Buy a multi-day pass for Angkor Wat to get the best value for your visit

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Cambodia from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Cambodia is typically during May to September, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Cambodia cost from the UK?

Flights: £600–£900 return. Hotels: £10–£30 per night. Beer (Angkor): £0.50–£1

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Cambodia?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Cambodia?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Cambodia holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Cambodia due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
