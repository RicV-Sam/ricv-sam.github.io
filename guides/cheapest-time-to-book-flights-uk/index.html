Cheapest Time To Book Flights From The UK

Finding the cheapest flights from the UK is not just about luck. Timing, seasonality and booking strategy can all have a major impact on the price you pay.

Best Time To Book Flights

  • Short haul Europe: 6–8 weeks before travel
  • Long haul destinations: 8–16 weeks before travel
  • Peak holiday periods: Book 4–6 months early

Cheapest Days To Fly

  • Tuesday and Wednesday are often cheapest
  • Early morning or late night flights cost less
  • Avoid Friday evening and Sunday returns

Extra Budget Flight Tips

  • Use flight comparison websites to track prices.
  • Be flexible with your departure airport.
  • Consider "hidden city" ticketing or open-jaw flights for long-haul.
  • Sign up for airline newsletters for flash sale alerts.

By combining these strategies, you can significantly reduce the cost of your next trip from the UK.
