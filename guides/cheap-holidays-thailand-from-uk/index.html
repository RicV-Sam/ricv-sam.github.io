Cheap Holidays To Thailand From The UK

1 min read

Thailand remains one of the world's premier budget destinations. For UK travellers, it offers a blend of vibrant city life in Bangkok, lush mountains in the north, and pristine beaches in the south—all at a fraction of the cost of European holidays.

The flight duration from the UK is approximately 11–13 hours direct, but choosing a flight with a short layover can often save you over £200 on your ticket price.

Cheapest Time To Visit Thailand

The cheapest months to visit Thailand from the UK are:

  • May to June
  • September to October

These months coincide with the low season, where you can find luxury resorts at budget prices.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Heathrow
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £550–£850 return
  • Hotels: £10–£35 per night
  • Street food meal: £1.50–£3

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Stick to street food for the best flavor and price
  • Use the BTS Skytrain or river boats in Bangkok
  • Island hop using local ferries instead of speedboats
  • Book internal flights with low-cost carriers like AirAsia
