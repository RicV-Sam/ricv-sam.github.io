Cheap All Inclusive Holidays To Italy From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Italy might be famous for its boutique hotels and fine dining, but it also offers excellent all-inclusive options, particularly on the islands of Sicily and Sardinia and along the Adriatic coast. These resorts are perfect for enjoying Italian hospitality and cuisine with a fixed budget.

All-inclusive in Italy often focuses heavily on the quality of the local food and wine, providing a truly authentic taste of the country.

Cheapest Time To Visit Italy

The cheapest months to visit Italy from the UK are:

  • October to March
  • May and June

Spring and autumn are the best times to visit, offering pleasant weather for both beach relaxation and exploring nearby historic sites.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£140 return
  • All-inclusive packages: £500–£850
  • Gelato (if not all-in): £3

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Look for 'Villaggi' (holiday villages) which offer great value all-inclusive for families
  • Fly with low-cost carriers to airports like Palermo, Catania or Alghero
  • Choose resorts that include beach service (umbrellas and loungers) in the price
  • Use the local trains (Trenitalia) for very affordable and scenic day trips

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Italy from the UK?

The cheapest time to visit Italy is typically during October to March and May and June, when flight and hotel prices are lower than the peak summer months.

How much does a holiday to Italy cost from the UK?

Flights: £40–£140 return. All-inclusive packages: £500–£850. Gelato (if not all-in): £3

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Italy?

London Gatwick, Stansted, Manchester, and Birmingham are common low-cost airports offering affordable routes to the area.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Italy?

Booking 6–10 weeks in advance typically offers the best balance of price and availability for Italy holidays.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Package holidays often offer better value to Italy due to bulk pricing and consumer protection, though DIY booking can occasionally save money during sales.
