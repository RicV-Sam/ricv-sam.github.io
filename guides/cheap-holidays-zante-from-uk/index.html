Cheap Holidays To Zante (Zakynthos) From The UK

3 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Exploring Zante on a budget is not only possible but incredibly rewarding for savvy travellers from the UK. Whether your heart is set on witnessing the iconic Navagio Shipwreck, exploring the depths of the stunning Blue Caves, or experiencing the turtle-nesting Gerakas Beach, you'll find that this destination caters exceptionally well to the cost-conscious. The accessibility provided by a wide range of airline partners means that finding a deal to Zante is often just a matter of timing and a bit of research.

Once you've touched down, Zante reveals its true value through its affordable infrastructure and local charm. The availability of emerald waters and vibrant social scenes ensures that every day is filled with wonder, regardless of your daily spend. By engaging with the local culture and choosing to stay in traditional neighbourhoods, you'll gain a deeper appreciation for Zante while enjoying a holiday that is as affordable as it is unforgettable. Cheapest Time To Visit Zante

The cheapest months to visit Zante from the UK are:

  • May and June
  • September

Early summer is particularly pleasant as the island is lush and the prices are at their most competitive.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Newcastle

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £60–£160 return
  • Hotels: £30–£65 per night
  • Beer in Laganas: £2.50

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Book a boat trip to Shipwreck Cove from a local harbor for better prices
  • Stay in self-catering accommodation and shop at local bakeries
  • Rent a small car or quad bike to explore the quieter northern coast
  • Avoid the main tourist strip restaurants for better value and more authentic food

Best Areas to Stay in Zante for Budget Travellers

Finding the perfect base in Zante doesn't have to break the bank if you know where to look. For the best deals, focus your search on up-and-coming neighbourhoods like the bustling Zante Town, which offer a great selection of budget accommodation. These spots are typically just a short hop from the main tourist zones, providing the perfect balance between convenience and cost-effectiveness.

Furthermore, districts like the lively Laganas and the family-friendly Tsilivi are renowned for their student populations and local markets, which translates to affordable beds and even cheaper food. By choosing to stay in these lively, authentic areas, you'll benefit from a more immersive experience in Zante while keeping your accommodation costs remarkably low. It's an excellent strategy for anyone looking to see the best of Zante on a strict budget.

Typical Daily Travel Costs in Zante

To enjoy Zante without overspending, a daily budget of approximately £50 and £80 is usually sufficient for the average backpacker or budget tourist. This figure accounts for basic accommodation, local transport, and satisfying meals from local eateries. It's a great destination for those who want a rich cultural experience without the high price tags found in other global hotspots, provided you make smart choices about where you eat and how you move around.

Transport costs in Zante are often one of the lowest parts of a traveller's budget, thanks to an extensive network of affordable local options. Similarly, food costs can be kept low by visiting local markets and bakeries for breakfast and lunch. By allocating your funds wisely and prioritizing the sights that matter most to you, you'll find that Zante offers incredible value for every pound spent.
