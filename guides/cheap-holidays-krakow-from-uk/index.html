Cheap Holidays To Krakow From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Krakow is a stunning medieval city that offers some of the best value for money in Europe. A 2.5-hour flight from the UK takes you to the heart of Poland, where history and culture are accessible on any budget.

The city's Main Market Square is one of the largest in Europe, and the nearby Jewish district of Kazimierz is perfect for finding affordable dining and unique nightlife.

Cheapest Time To Visit Krakow

January, February, and November are the cheapest months to visit Krakow from the UK.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Luton
  • Manchester
  • Liverpool
  • Edinburgh

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£120 return
  • Hotels: £55–£130 per night
  • City break packages: £180–£350

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Eat at 'Milk Bars' (Bar Mleczny) for traditional, cheap food
  • Take a free walking tour to learn about the city's history
  • Walk along the Vistula river for great views of Wawel Castle
  • Use the frequent and cheap train from the airport to the centre

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Krakow from the UK?

January and February offer the lowest prices for flights and accommodation.

How much does a holiday to Krakow cost from the UK?

Flights: £40–£120 return. Hotels: £55–£130 per night. City break packages: £180–£350

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Krakow?

Krakow John Paul II International (KRK) is the main gateway, with many low-cost options.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Krakow?

Booking 8 weeks in advance typically gets you the best value on well-located hotels.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

DIY booking is very easy and often the cheapest way to experience Krakow.
