Cheap Holidays To Santorini From The UK

3 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Santorini from the UK is often seen as the pinnacle of luxury Greek travel, but this volcanic masterpiece is surprisingly accessible for those who know how to navigate its caldera. Whether you're dreaming of the iconic blue-domed churches of Oia or the unique red sands of Akrotiri, Santorini offers a world-class aesthetic that respects a modest budget. For UK travellers, the abundance of direct budget flights during the summer season makes it a prime candidate for a 2026 value island break.

The secret to a cheap Santorini trip is basing yourself away from the high-priced caldera edge. Beyond the expensive sunset terraces, the island reveals its true value through its traditional villages and local wineries. If you enjoy Greek island hopping on a budget, you might also like cheap holidays to Crete. By staying in coastal hubs like Perissa or Kamari and utilizing the excellent local bus network, you can experience the full magic of Santorini's sunsets and volcanic scenery while keeping your holiday costs firmly under control.

Cheapest Time To Visit Santorini

April, May, and October are the best months for finding cheap holidays to Santorini.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Newcastle

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £75–£185 return from major UK airports
  • Hotels: £60–£135 per night for a guesthouse or beachside hotel
  • Package holidays: £380–£680 for a 7-night sun break

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Base Yourself in the East: Stay in Perissa or Kamari. These beach resorts offer significantly lower prices for accommodation and dining than Fira or Oia, and are just a short bus ride from the caldera.
  • Use the Local KTEL Bus: Don't rent a car or take expensive taxis. The local bus network is efficient, air-conditioned, and connects all major villages and beaches for just a few Euros.
  • Picnic at Sunset: Instead of paying for a "sunset view" table at a restaurant, grab some local wine and gyros from a bakery and find a spot on the walls of Oia Castle for the same world-class view for free.
  • Eat Gyros for Lunch: A filling pita gyro from a local grill house costs around €4-€6 and is the most delicious and affordable way to fuel your island explorations.

Best Areas to Stay in Santorini for Budget Travellers

To keep your costs down and still enjoy the best of the island, consider these budget-friendly locations:

  • Perissa/Perivolos: Famous for its black sand beach, this area offers the highest concentration of affordable hostels, guesthouses, and value-for-money tavernas on the island.
  • Kamari: Another great beachside hub with a beautiful promenade, offering a wide range of mid-range and budget hotels that are often much cheaper than caldera-side options.
  • Karterados: A traditional village just a 15-minute walk from the capital, Fira. It offers a more authentic local vibe and significantly lower accommodation prices while staying centrally located.

Typical Daily Travel Costs in Santorini

To enjoy Santorini without overspending, a daily budget of approximately £80 and £130 is usually sufficient for the average backpacker or budget tourist. This figure accounts for basic accommodation, local transport, and satisfying meals from local eateries. It's a great destination for those who want a rich cultural experience without the high price tags found in other global hotspots, provided you make smart choices about where you eat and how you move around. For more Greek inspiration, see our overall guide to budget Greece.

Transport costs in Santorini are often one of the lowest parts of a traveller's budget, thanks to an extensive network of affordable local options. Similarly, food costs can be kept low by visiting local markets and bakeries for breakfast and lunch. By allocating your funds wisely and prioritizing the sights that matter most to you, you'll find that Santorini offers incredible value for every pound spent.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Santorini from the UK?

April to May and the month of October are the most affordable times to visit.

How much does a holiday to Santorini cost from the UK?

Flights: £90–£200 return. Hotels: £70–£140 per night. All-inclusive packages: £450–£650

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Santorini?

Santorini Thira Airport (JTR) receives direct flights from several UK cities during the summer season.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Santorini?

Booking 6 months in advance is wise for Santorini due to its immense popularity and limited budget accommodation.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Packages can often save you money on Santorini by bundling flights with hotels that are otherwise expensive.
