Cheap Holidays To Santorini From The UK

1 min read

Published by Budget Holidays Hub — UK travel research and budget holiday guides.

Santorini is famous for its stunning sunsets and blue-domed churches, and while it's a luxury favourite, it can be enjoyed on a budget. A 4-hour flight from the UK brings you to this volcanic paradise.

By choosing to stay in the beach resorts of Perissa or Kamari instead of the cliffside towns of Oia or Fira, travellers can enjoy the island's beauty at a fraction of the cost.

Cheapest Time To Visit Santorini

April, May, and October are the best months for finding cheap holidays to Santorini.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Gatwick
  • London Stansted
  • Manchester
  • Birmingham
  • Newcastle

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £90–£200 return
  • Hotels: £70–£140 per night
  • All-inclusive packages: £450–£650

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Stay in Perissa or Kamari for cheaper hotels and food
  • Use the excellent local bus system to get around the island
  • Buy your own wine and enjoy the sunset from a public viewpoint
  • Visit the archaeological site of Akrotiri

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest time to visit Santorini from the UK?

April to May and the month of October are the most affordable times to visit.

How much does a holiday to Santorini cost from the UK?

Flights: £90–£200 return. Hotels: £70–£140 per night. All-inclusive packages: £450–£650

Which UK airports offer the cheapest flights to Santorini?

Santorini Thira Airport (JTR) receives direct flights from several UK cities during the summer season.

How far in advance should I book a cheap holiday to Santorini?

Booking 6 months in advance is wise for Santorini due to its immense popularity and limited budget accommodation.

Are package holidays cheaper than booking separately?

Packages can often save you money on Santorini by bundling flights with hotels that are otherwise expensive.
