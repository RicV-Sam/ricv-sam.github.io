Cheap Holidays To Portugal From The UK | Budget Travel Guide

1 min read

Portugal has long been a favourite for British holidaymakers seeking affordable sun, sea, and sand. The Algarve region, with its stunning cliffs and golden beaches, remains the primary draw, but cities like Lisbon and Porto offer incredible value for culture-seekers. Known for its laid-back atmosphere and excellent seafood, Portugal provides a high quality of life at a price point that is often lower than its Mediterranean neighbours.

With frequent low-cost flights from across the UK to Faro and Lisbon, airline competition is fierce, ensuring that even last-minute travellers can find a bargain. Package holidays typically range from £300 to £600 per person, making it an accessible option for families and couples alike. Whether you are exploring the historic streets of Albufeira or enjoying a city break in the capital, Portugal’s combination of reliability and affordability is hard to beat.

Cheapest Time To Visit Portugal

The cheapest months to visit Portugal from the UK are:

  • March to June
  • September to October

These months avoid the peak summer rush and provide the best balance of pleasant weather and lower accommodation costs.

Cheapest Airports To Fly From The UK

  • London Stansted
  • London Luton
  • Manchester
  • Bristol
  • East Midlands

Typical Holiday Costs

  • Flights: £40–£150 return
  • Hotels: £50–£110 per night
  • Package holidays: £300–£600

Budget Tips To Save Money

  • Book at least 3 months in advance for the best flight deals
  • Use the efficient and affordable train network between Lisbon and the Algarve
  • Look for "Prato do Dia" (Plate of the Day) in local restaurants for cheap, authentic meals
  • Consider staying in Faro or Lagos rather than the more expensive resort towns
